Hummus

Description

This is a quick and easy way to make hummus at home. Once cooked, simply put everything into a food processor, and enjoy some deliciously smooth hummus.

You can even customize this recipe, adding your favorite spices and/or toppings!

Ingredients

  • 6 cups water
  • 2 cups dried chickpeas
  • 1 cup olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic peeled
  • 1/4 cup tahini
  • 1/2 cup water
  • juice of 2 lemons
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Place chickpeas and water in the Cooking Pot. Secure the lid. Make sure the steam release valve is in the “Seal” (closed) position.
  2. Press BEANS/CHILI, set the pressure to HIGH, and adjust the time to 45 minutes. Press START/STOP
  3. Once cooking has completed, and the pressure has released, set aside to cool. Drain
  4. Place all remaining ingredients in food processor and process to form a smooth paste.
  5. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
