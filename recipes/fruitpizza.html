Fruit Pizza

Description

This delicious fruit pizza has a sugar cookie crust with a smooth cream cheese frosting. You can customize it with any type of sliced fruit that you prefer!

You can also get creative with the pattern of fruit. If you prefer to save some time, you can use store-bought sugar cookie dough instead of making your own.

Ingredients

  • Sugar Cookie Crust
    • 3/4 cup sugar
    • 1 1/4 cup flour
    • 1/4 tsp baking powder
    • 1/4 tsp salt
    • 1/2 cup butter softened
    • 1 tsp vanilla extract
    • 1 large egg
  • Cream Cheese Frosting
    • 12 oz cream cheese softened
    • 1/4 cup butter softened
    • 1 tsp vanilla extract
    • 1 1/4 cup powdered sugar
  • Fruit Toppings
    • 7-10 strawberries sliced
    • 5-6 kiwis sliced
    • 1 mango sliced
    • 1/2 cup blueberries
    • 1 small bunch of grapes halved

Steps

  1. Make the Crust
    1. Mix the butter, sugar, egg, and vanilla until well combined. (You can use an electric mixer or just mix with a spoon if your butter is melted.
    2. Add flour, baking powder, and salt. Mix until combined.
    3. Chill the dough for 30 minutes.
  2. Bake the Crust
    1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.
    2. Grease a 14-inch pizza pan or round baking stone, or line with parchment.
    3. Roll the chilled dough out onto it, leaving some space around the edge.
    4. Bake for 12 minutes. Allow it to cool.
  3. Make the Cream Cheese Frosting
    1. Using an electric mixer, combine the cream cheese, butter, powdered sugar and vanilla until smooth and creamy.
    2. Spread over the cooled cookie crust and chill again to firm up the frosting.
    3. Top with fruit!
