Stuffed Shells

Description

This is an easy recipe for baked pasta shells, stuffed with a mix of cheeses and topped with marinara sauce. This recipe is easily customizable, so feel free to add sauteed greens, sausage, or any other ingredients if you so choose.

Ingredients

  • 20 jumbo pasta shells
  • 2/3 lb ricotta cheese (about 2 heaping cups)
  • 2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese divided
  • 8 tsp finely chopped parsley leaves
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp ground black pepper
  • 2/3 of 24 oz jar marinara sauce (3 cups, divided)
  • 4 tsp finely grated cheese (Parmesan or Romano)

Steps

  1. Arrange a rack in the middle of the oven and heat to 375Â°F.
  2. Meanwhile, bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the shells and cook according to package directions. Drain,then briefly rinse with cold water until cool enough to handle.
  3. Place the following ingredients in a large bowl, and stir to combine:
    • ricotta
    • 2 cups of mozzarella
    • parsley
    • salt and pepper
    Taste and add more salt, if needed.
  4. Spread 1 cup of the sauce on the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  5. Stuff each shell with about 1 heaping tablespoon of the mozzarella filling and place filling-side up in the dish. If some shells split during the cooking process, you may have a few fragments left over, but you should end up with about 2 dozen shells in the dish.
  6. Top the shells evenly with the remaining 2 cups sauce. Sprinkle with the remaining 1 cup mozzarella and Parmesan or Pecorino.
  7. Bake uncovered until the cheese is melted and the sauce is bubbling, about 30 minutes.
